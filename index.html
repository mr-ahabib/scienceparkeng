About Science Park Engineering

Top Laboratory Equipment Provider in Bangladesh

Science Park Engineering is a leading provider, importer, and supplier of laboratory equipment in Bangladesh. We specialize in offering a wide range of scientific instruments, chemicals, reagents, and consumables to sectors such as pharmaceuticals, universities, research institutes, dyeing and knitting industries, and more.

Our Product Portfolio Includes:

  • Laboratory equipment and instruments like chromatography systems, testing meters, lab glassware, and plasticware.
  • Textile testing equipment, pharmaceutical instruments, raw materials, and industrial QC products.
  • Kits and tools for chemistry, biology, microbiology, physics, chemical and water testing, environmental studies, and fisheries.

Customer satisfaction is the driving force behind our operations. As a committed service provider, we aim to grow alongside our clients by consistently meeting their demands and delivering reliable solutions.
